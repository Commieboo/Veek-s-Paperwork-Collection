This document details a trade agreement between station [bold]Mordheim CS-550, Cybersun Industries[/bold] and [bold]Edge PR-162, Nanotrasen[/bold]. The former party offers a total of [bold]20000 spesos[/bold] of upfront payment to the logistics account in exchange for [bold]40% of final station earnings at the end of the shift.[/bold]
Along with this agreement, the Cybersun agent receives all legal rights of a foreign visitor, as well as the authorization to carry their personal protective clothing, radio key, non-lethal technology and inquire about logistics funds at any point during the shift.

Final station earning is considered to be the leftover station funds at the request console or in personal posession of the Logistic Officer in the moment when the crew exchange shuttle is called, or an early evacuation protocol is initiated. Token of trust is given to the client as to not have them hide or otherwise remove the money prematurely to decrease profit gained.

Should the agreement be broken:

[italic]* By Nanotrasen party, by directly injuring the deployed executive, intentionally wasting or withdrawing money before the deal can be finalized or otherwise preventing logistics from doing their job. The executive holds all rights to ask for the full initial payment back.
[]* By Cybersun party, by sabotaging the station's efforts, attempting to steal or withdraw the money prematurely or otherwise cause direct harm with enough evidence. Nanotrasen holds all rights to keep the initial money invested without having to pay the agreed precentage.[/italic]

[color=orange][bold]This document is only valid when stamped and signed by both parties.[/bold][/color]
