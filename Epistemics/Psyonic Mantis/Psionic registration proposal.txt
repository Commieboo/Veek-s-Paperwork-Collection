Due to a series of increasingly worrying reports from neighboring stations regarding Psionic users i propose the establishment of a registry of Psionic beings;

🛈 This registry shall be managed by the Psionic Mantis and registered Psionic beings shall be granted a registry card which will denote their Psionic registration type. Psionic users will be registered using the following 'Type notation' ;

​​​​​​​​​​​​​   🛈 [italic][color=gray]Type A denotes psionic abilities that are deemed non-harmfull and allows the free use of Psionic abilities [/italic][/color]
​​​​​​​​​​​​​   🛈 [italic][color=gray]Type B denotes psionic abilities that may pose a threat to the station or the user in some circumstances and limits the use of said powers to authorized use only, authorized by the user's department head. [/italic][/color]
​​​​​​​​​​​​​   🛈 [italic][color=gray]Type C denotes psionic abilities that are considered a threat to the station and the user and orders a complete ban of the users abilities, the Psionic Mantis may take any required steps to halt the users ability to use said power.[/italic][/color]

🛈 Psionic beings that refuse registration or otherwise refuse their Type notation shall be held liable to Endangerment of the crew and prosecuted as such.
