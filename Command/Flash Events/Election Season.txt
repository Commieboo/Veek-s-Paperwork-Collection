[head=3]• To promote employee engagement and the feeling of agency i propose we create a new legislative body for the station. [/head]

• This body will be comprised of four elected employees of the station and the acting captain of the station. 

• The employees wanting to join this council will have until 00:40 to publicly announce their bid for election by informing the Chief-Justice or the Head of Personnel, possible candidates are free to promote and advocate for their position within the council. 

• The Four employees will be elected in a public vote held in a common area of the station at 00:50 shift time by all members present during the election. 

• The new legislative body will have the ability to propose and vote on new adjustments on the SoP, as long as these changes do not violate space law or cause undue harm to the station.

When a new adjustment is agreed upon by the council it will be announced to the station and adopted.

​​​​​​​​​​​​​   🛈 [color=Gray]Members of this council are still bound by space law.[/color]
