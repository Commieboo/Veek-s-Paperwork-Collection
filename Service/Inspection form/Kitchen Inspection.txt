[bold]Major Violations:[/bold]
(   ) Live animals allowed to roam the kitchen
(   ) Organs/carcasses/rotting meat present
(   ) Excessive blood on the floor in food prep areas
(   ) Unsafe chemicals in food prep areas (gasoline, meth, etc.)
(   ) Radioactive materials present

All major violations must be rectified as soon as possible.
If more than one major violation is present, the kitchen must not be allowed to serve food until violations are rectified.
Any major violations warrants an automatic F score.
