# Declaration of Independence
[color=#1ba569]░░▒▒░░ [head=2]Royal Message of Queen Veek-Beem.[/head]
▐████▌ [head=3]Declaration of Independence [/head]
▄█░░█▄ [head=3]From the sovereign monarchy of Veek. [/head]
[/color]──────────────────────────────────────────
    The royal crown of Veek-Beem would like to inform the station that the territory formerly known as permanent containment or 'Perma' is now the sovereign territory of the Veek monarchy.

All subjects within 'Perma' now fall under the sovereignty of the Veek monarchy. 

The crown and it's subjects shall adhere to it's policy of non-expansionism and self-isolation for as long as the demands of the crown are met.

──────────────────────────────────────────
Sincerely, Queen Veek-Beem
